Everything I use daily for software engineering, writing, and building with AI.

## Editor & Terminal

- **Neovim** — My primary editor. Fast, extensible, and stays out of the way.
- **VS Code** — I reach for it occasionally, mostly for visual debugging or when pairing with someone who prefers it.
- **Claude Code** — My AI coding companion in the terminal. I use it for everything from scaffolding features to code review.
- **tmux** — Terminal multiplexer for managing sessions and splits. Essential for my Neovim workflow.

## OS & System

- **macOS** — My daily driver for development.
- **zsh** — Default shell, customized with aliases and prompt tweaks.

## Apps & Productivity

- **Arc Browser** — My primary browser. The workspace and tab management keep things organized.

## Audio/Video

- **Shure Digital Microphone** — Clear audio for calls and recordings.
- **Sony WH-1000XM5** — Noise-cancelling headphones for deep focus work.
- **AirPods Pro** — For quick calls and when I'm away from the desk.

## Desk Setup

- **UHK (Ultimate Hacking Keyboard)** — Split ergonomic keyboard. The split layout keeps my shoulders relaxed during long coding sessions.
- **Ultrawide Monitor** — Plenty of screen real estate for side-by-side code and terminal.
- **Autonomous Standing Desk** — Sit-stand desk that I alternate throughout the day.
